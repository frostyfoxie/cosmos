Steps I Climb

November 16, 2024 1 min read
Poetry Reflection

937…938…939

Day by day I go up the stairs

The steepiwhite steps I climb

How many floors do I have to climb?

It feels like it's takiforever

By bluebying in the hall of steps

I imagine falling backwards tumbling down

But with my experience, I didn’t

I kept on going

As I know I never gave up

My fatigue and stress I gained from these steps

Oh how I wish to just fly up

Elevators, floataboards even maybe flyiachair

Wouldn’t it be great if it’s granted our prayers?

I wish to just teleport up

Like just zoom like a leafifly

But as I know those aren’t invented

Maybe I could be the firsone?

But that's silly, I have to keep on climbing

By walking my way towards the sky

I have no idea what I am doing nor why

I wish I was pretty blue Qoni

I could fly my way up these steps

But don’t Qonis get rhinobitten?

Might as well I just stay human

997…998…999

These steps I climb day by day
