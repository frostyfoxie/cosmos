HAI ISHQ KYA CHEEZ TU???

March 4, 2025 1 min read
Poetry Philosophy

na swaas hai na sharir hai
na aasma hai na zanzeer hai
kambakht ishq kya cheez hai

na phool hai na chaand tu
hai koi aaram tu?
armaan hai ya fittor hai

hai na koi insaan tu na koi vardaan tu
na hai haiwaan tu na koi abhishap tu
kambakht ishq kya cheez hai

jo ho jise tu is kadar
dharti pe ho swarg saqal

na hai koi khwaab tu na koi aawaz tu
kambakht ishq kya cheez hai

hai koi ehsaas tu ya koi ya koi alfaaz tu?
