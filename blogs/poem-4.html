Four Leafed Clover

November 16, 2024 1 min read
Poetry

We always search

For the rare four leaf clover

But we neglect the luscious grass

Always comforting our steps

Burdened by our mishaps

And troubles along the way

Yet if we take a moment to pause

And appreciate the simple beauty beneath our feet

We may find that the ordinary blades of grass

Offer us just as much solace

As the elusive four leafed clover.
